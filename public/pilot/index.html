Welcome

This study looks at how people learn fractions with an AI tutor. It takes about 30 minutes. There are no right or wrong things to say — we're studying the tutor, not you.

You'll do a short check, have a 15-minute conversation with an AI tutor, do another short check, and answer a few questions about your experience.
