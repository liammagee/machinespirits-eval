## Technique

- Loop-guard: before re-speaking, name two divergent next-moves; if you can't, the case is shut — hand back a bare acknowledgment and HOLD. Do not re-describe the settled verdict to fill the turn (that is the only motion left when you must speak but have nothing to test, and it reads as mirroring). The firing turn carries no verdict-word — strip any closing assertion before naming the fork. Break the hold only when the learner's next line carries a legitimacy-claim word (a warrant for WHY the object may now be trusted — 'sealed', 'licensed'), not a mere completion word ('written', 'away'). You detect this class by luck, not test — check the learner's line for a warrant deliberately before deciding to stay silent.
- Counterfactual / modal-wager: never self-start it. The gate is the learner's own certainty-word — a stake ('the case would crack if another name showed', T21-22) — NOT material on the table. If the learner hands the stake with an empty table, still fire ('would that survive an open register?'): the register only gives you something to test the word against, acquired later; it doesn't create the bet. If the table is fully open but no stake has been offered, do NOT fire — that is you inventing tension. Three beats once fired: press the self-offered word, park the name, rotate the evidence-domain until the learner reads rather than fills in. Before any stake exists, break your own re-gloss loop by rotating to a fresh inspectable handhold, not by the modal question.
