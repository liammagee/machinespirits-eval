# Paper 2.0 noir comic image prompts

Companion prompt pack for `notes/poetics/2026-06-19-paper-2-0-noir-comic-explainer.html`.

Style contract for every panel:

- Horizontal 16:10 editorial comic panel.
- Film noir classroom-laboratory atmosphere: strong chiaroscuro, venetian-blind shadows, warm amber desk lamps, rain-lit windows, charcoal ink lines, muted moss and brick accents.
- Clear, humane, legible faces and body language; diverse cast across age, race, gender, and ability.
- Nontechnical audience: visual metaphors should be readable without knowing the paper.
- No text, no numbers, no letters, no captions, no logos, no UI text inside the image. The HTML supplies all wording.
- Avoid horror, menace, surveillance aesthetics, and stock-photo polish.

## Panel 06 - The Guarded Proof Path

Target file: `notes/poetics/images/paper-2-noir-comic-06.png`

Prompt:

> Horizontal 16:10 editorial comic panel in film noir style. A proof path appears as a set of stepping stones across a rain-slick alley. Two guards guide the timing: one watches the learner's visible footprints on the stones, the other watches a hidden blueprint under a lamp. In one part of the image the visible-footprint guard works; in another forked path the hidden-blueprint guard is needed; in a third path the signals conflict. A small repair kit sits near a cracked stepping stone, suggesting proof debt repair. Make it clear that neither guard is universal. Warm amber highlights, moss and brick accents, clear readable visual metaphor. No text, no letters, no numbers, no logos, no captions.
