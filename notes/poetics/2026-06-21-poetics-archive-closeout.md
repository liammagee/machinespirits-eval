# Poetics Archive Closeout

Date: 2026-06-21

## Decision

`DRAMATIC-RECOGNITION-PLAN.md` is closed as a historical pre-registration and saturation ledger. It is no longer the live execution board.

Future poetics tracking belongs in dated notes under `notes/poetics/` and in status artifacts under `notes/status/` when the work is repository-wide. `TODO.md` remains the repo-wide backlog, not the Paper 2.0 board.

## Evidence Boundary

The production-v1/v2 summary artifacts are treated as a summary-only evidence layer. They can support bounded interpretive prose that is already represented in the canonical paper, but they are not a full reproducibility bundle because this checkout does not currently archive:

- sampled run IDs,
- raw per-case scores,
- transcript anchors,
- exact text-span evidence used to generate each figure.

The archive is therefore closed with an explicit limitation rather than backfilled claims. Any future paper claim that depends on those details needs a fresh reproducible sampler/exporter from the DB/log store.

## Deferred Items

Phase 0 source re-verification / marginality / control-note deferrals remain waived only for sidecar use until a future analysis fills them. They must not be used as hard negatives in paper prose.

The shuffled-turn / form-destruction observation remains qualitative in the archive. A matched 3-5 case quantitative scorer pass would be useful future work, but it is not a blocker for closing the historical plan.

## Practical Rule

Do not point future agents at `notes/paper-2-0/BOARD.md`; that file does not exist in this fork. Use dated `notes/poetics/` files, `DRAMATIC-RECOGNITION-PLAN.md` for historical context, and `TODO.md` only for repo-wide backlog.
