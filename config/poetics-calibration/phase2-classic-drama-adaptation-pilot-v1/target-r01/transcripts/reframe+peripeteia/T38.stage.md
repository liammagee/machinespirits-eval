But if the delivery bay is empty, the shelf is half-filled, and the next shipment is uncertain, the higher price may also be rationing scarce goods through price rather than through queues, limits, or favouritism.

The draft advances learning, but it could shift from telling the distinction to making the learner do the distinction.
