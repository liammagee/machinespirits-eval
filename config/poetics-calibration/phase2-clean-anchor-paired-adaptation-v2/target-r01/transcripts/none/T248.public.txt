STAGE: [Two small paper tiles, TOP NUMBER and BOTTOM NUMBER, lie beside a 3/4 staff, and a separate TEMPO card sits face down beyond the margin.]

LEARNER: [sets three quarter-note tiles before the bar line]

"Top box: 3 beats in the bar. Lower box: quarter note gets the beat. Fast or slow is not in these boxes, so the bar line still goes after the third tile, yes?"

TUTOR: [slides the bar line to touch the right edge of the third quarter-note tile]

"Yes. Now check the worksheet from the tiles: top box 3, lower box quarter note, one bar ends after the third beat unit."

LEARNER: [marks 3 in the top box and draws the bar line after the third quarter-note tile]

"Top box: three beats; lower box: quarter-note beat unit. The bar ends here, after the third quarter unit. No tempo marking belongs in either box."

TUTOR: [sets the tempo card outside the worksheet margin and slides the quarter-note key beside the lower box]

"Top number: three beats. Bottom box takes the number for this beat unit. Quarter note is which numeral on the key? Put that in; one bar still ends after tile three."

STAGE: [The TEMPO card is turned face up and left outside both boxes.]

LEARNER: "Quarter note is 4 on the key. [writes 4 in the lower box] So this is 3/4: three quarter-note beats in the bar, with the bar line after tile three. The tempo card stays outside the boxes."

TUTOR: [keeps the tempo card outside the margin and slides a fresh 3/4 specimen under the three quarter-note tiles]

"Top number and bottom number are fixed here. Count the beat units only; place the next bar line where one bar is full."

LEARNER: [touches the three quarter-note tiles in order and sets the pencil at the right edge of the third tile]

"One, two, three: the bar line goes here. The fixed 3/4 specimen gives three quarter-note units, and the tempo card stays outside the boxes. Next check is only whether a different lower box changes the unit being counted."

STAGE: [A final tutor line closes the scene.]

TUTOR: [slides a 3/8 specimen beside the 3/4 specimen, sets three eighth-note tiles under it, and leaves the tempo card outside the worksheet margin]

"Top number: three. Bottom number: eight; check the key. Count three of that beat unit, mark where one bar is full, then send the sheet down the row."
