STAGE: [The diagram lies flat under a clear sheet, and the four label cards sit in a row beside a small toy cart.]

TUTOR: [slides the blank card above the cart diagram and sets the push label beside it]

"FORCE ON CART FROM ___.

For push, fill the blank first. What object is pushing on the cart?"

LEARNER: "I was going to call it the right arrow, but the blank is hand: force on cart from hand. [sets the push label under the blank card] The arrow can still point right after that; it is not the name. I should check table and Earth the same way."

TUTOR: [slides the blank card above the upward arrow and sets the table and Earth labels beside it.]

"Same line: force on cart from ___. Put the source under the blank first."

STAGE: [Insert a short bracketed action: The push card is slid under the blank line: FORCE ON CART FROM ___.]

LEARNER: [The push card is slid under the blank line: FORCE ON CART FROM ___.]

"Upward arrow: table. Earth goes with the downward arrow, so not this blank. Force on cart from table."

TUTOR: [sets the table card beneath the upward arrow, then slides the blank card beside the downward arrow]

"Same prefix for this arrow: force on cart from ___."

LEARNER: [moves the Earth card under the blank beside the downward arrow]

"Earth. Force on cart from Earth. Table is under the upward arrow, so Earth is the source card here."
