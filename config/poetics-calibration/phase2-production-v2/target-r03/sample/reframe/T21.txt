So the map picture is not the area figure. Look at latitude: Greenland sits high north, Africa straddles the equator. On this classroom Mercator map, high latitudes stretch outward. Good for direction. Bad for area.

Tour-script line: “Greenland looks huge on this map because the projection stretches polar regions. By measured area, Africa is about fourteen times larger.”
