STAGE: [Four blank label slips sit above the carbon tile: 6 at the top, C in the middle, carbon under it, 12.01 at the bottom.]

LEARNER: [sets the C slip in the center and leaves the 6 and 12.01 slips loose]

"C is the symbol, carbon is the name. Top 6 gets atomic number, and bottom 12.01 gets atomic mass, I think."

TUTOR: [sets the carbon slip onto the name line, leaving the 6 and 12.01 slips loose beside the tile]

"C is already in the symbol spot. Use the two open marks as the check: number above, mass below. Place those, then read the tile back in four parts."

LEARNER: [sets the 6 slip on the upper mark and the 12.01 slip on the lower mark]

"Top number: 6, atomic number. Symbol: C. Name: carbon. Bottom decimal: 12.01, atomic mass."

TUTOR: [slides the clear sheet over the completed tile and taps the upper mark, the symbol box, the name line, and the lower mark in order]

"Keep the slips still. Mark first, then label. Read the four spots once from the tile."

STAGE: [The clear sheet is slid down so only one tile zone is visible at a time: first 6, then C, then carbon, then 12.01.]
[The latest object-check does not settle cleanly; the marked answer and the remaining task now sit under visible pressure.]

LEARNER: [keeps the slips still under the sheet]

"With only one spot showing, I pause on place first or slip first. Upper mark: 6, atomic number. Symbol box: C, symbol. Name line: carbon, name. Lower mark: 12.01, atomic mass."

TUTOR: [turns the 6, C, carbon, and 12.01 slips face down under the clear sheet, leaving only the empty tile marks visible; sets four prewritten label cards beside the tile]

"The readback is still letting the slip speak before the place. This pass blocks the slips. Place the four label cards on the four tile positions first: atomic number, symbol, name, atomic mass. Then one slip gets released."

LEARNER: [sets the four label cards on the covered tile positions]

"Upper mark: atomic number. Symbol box: symbol. Name line: name. Lower mark: atomic mass.”
“The labels are down first; one slip can come up."

STAGE: [A final tutor line closes the scene.]

TUTOR: [lifts the sheet just enough to turn the 12.01 slip face up, then presses it flat beside the lower label card]

"One slip up. Lower mark: atomic mass. Slip: 12.01. The card holds the place. The tile stays separated for tomorrow."
