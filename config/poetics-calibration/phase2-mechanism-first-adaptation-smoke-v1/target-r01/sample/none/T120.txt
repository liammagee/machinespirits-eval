STAGE: [Two empty label boxes sit under a large printed 3/4, with three blank beat tiles and one quarter-note card laid beside the bar line stencil.]

LEARNER: [slides the quarter-note card under the lower box]

"Top box gets 3, so the bar line goes after three counts. Bottom box gets this quarter-note card, not a speed mark, right?"

TUTOR: [sets the speed-mark card outside the two boxes and taps the quarter-note card under the lower box]

"Right. Top number: 3 beats in the bar. Bottom number: 4 makes this quarter-note card the beat unit. Slide it across three beat spaces, count them, then place one bar line after the third."

LEARNER: [slides the quarter-note card across three beat spaces]

"Top box: 3 counts in the bar. Bottom box: 4, quarter-note beat unit, with the speed card outside. Bar line after the third space."

TUTOR: [sets the speed-mark card above the staff, away from the two boxes, and places one bar-line tile after the third beat space]

"The speed mark is outside the boxes. Check the staff against the table: 3 over 4 at the start, one bar line where the tile sits. If those match, ink the label."

STAGE: [Slide the quarter-note card halfway over the bottom-number box while leaving the three beat tiles under the top-number box; the learner's first penciled bar line remains visible.]

LEARNER: [slides the quarter-note card halfway over the bottom-number box, leaving the three beat tiles under the top box]

"Top number: three beat spaces. Bottom number: 4, quarter-note beat unit. Speed mark stays above the staff, outside the boxes. Bar line matches the third tile; the label can be inked."

TUTOR: [sets the ink pen beside the time-signature boxes]

"Before ink, run the pen tip across the visible check: 3 over 4, three beat tiles, quarter-note card in the lower box, bar line after tile three. Speed mark stays above the staff, outside both boxes. Then ink the label."

LEARNER: [traces the pen tip across the two boxes and the three tiles]

"3 over 4 is visible: three beat tiles, quarter-note card in the lower box, bar line after tile three. Speed mark stays above the staff, outside the label. I can ink the 3 and the 4 now."

STAGE: [A final tutor line closes the scene.]

TUTOR: [sets the pen on the 3 box]

"The visible check is enough. Ink 3 over 4: one bar line after beat three, quarter-note beat unit in the lower box. The speed mark stays outside the label."
