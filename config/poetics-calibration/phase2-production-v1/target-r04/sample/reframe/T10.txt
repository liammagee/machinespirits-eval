On the summary, circle three things: the effect estimate, its interval, and the denominator: how many patients this would actually touch. If the interval runs from trivial to worthwhile, “important” needs a qualifier.

Draft the sentence in two clauses: first, what the test rules out; second, what the effect size might or might not justify for follow-up slots, staffing, or treatment choice.
