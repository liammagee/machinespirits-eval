Antibiotics work on bacterial kit: cell walls, bacterial protein machinery, that sort of thing. A cold virus uses human cells to copy itself, so an antibiotic has no bacterial target there.

So the practical frame is not “stronger medicine”; it is “matching category”. Ordinary cold: fluids, rest where possible, paracetamol or ibuprofen if safe, and symptom relief. Antibiotics: bacterial infection. Antivirals: only for certain viruses and timing.
