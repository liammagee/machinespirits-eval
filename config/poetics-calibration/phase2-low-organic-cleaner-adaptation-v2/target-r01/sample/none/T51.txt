"160, 160, 160. The curve still pulls like a route shape, but the number is not moving. This bent line is carrying one height from the legend."

"The arrow moved; the contour did not. Same printed line, same tile. Touch one new spot on the bend and read the legend number once more."

"160. Same printed line under the same tile. The bend is not changing the number."

"Same printed line, same 160. The dot is beside the contour, not on it. Touch the inked line once more and read its number; leave the dot blank."

LEARNER: [touches the inked bend, leaving the pencil dot alone]

"160. The dot stays blank; it is beside the line, not on the contour. The tile goes with the inked line."

STAGE: [A final tutor line closes the scene.]

TUTOR: [sets the 160 tile flat on the inked bend and caps the pencil]

"This contour connects equal elevation: 160 all along the ink. The legend gives height, not a route."
