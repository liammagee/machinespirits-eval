arXiv:2606.15783 [WATCH] — surfaced by the daily routine (2026-06-17-research-roundup.html).

The poetics pipeline currently scores each transcript on a single holistic rubric ( evaluation-rubric-poetics.yaml ). The SemEval Task 4 decomposition (theme / course-of-action / outcome) is nearly isomorphic to the three-act dramatic structure the rubric tries to capture. The multi-view alignment approach could provide a more principled way to compare transcripts from different cells on each dramatic dimension independently — a direct route to the inter-cell narrative-distance metric the poetics arc needs but currently lacks. The SemEval dataset itself (released with the task) may also serve as a synthetic training corpus for the critic model.
