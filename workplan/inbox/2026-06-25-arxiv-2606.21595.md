The poetics ontology (peripeteia / anagnorisis / hamartia / etc.) is currently a flat YAML schema. PEBM's per-entity calibration idea translates here: different dramatic concepts have different coverage in training corpora and therefore different confidence calibration under the critic LLM. The "Brand Hallucination Paradox" is directly observable in poetics scoring — anagnorisis may be confidently over-applied precisely because it is a prominent concept the model has seen many times, while rarer concepts like hamartia are under-detected. A per-concept calibration audit of the poetics critic would be a concrete near-term fix for κ divergence.
