The ego-superego deliberation cycle already produces rich artifact traces ( scores_with_reasoning , tutor_deliberation_* , learner_deliberation_* ) stored in the DB but never reused at inference. MATM suggests a retrieval layer: past superego critiques that matched a scenario can seed the current superego's prior, making revision more principled. This also directly addresses Finding 11 / D3 (insight-action gap) — agents that extract insight but don't act on it might do so if prior successful revisions were retrievable.
