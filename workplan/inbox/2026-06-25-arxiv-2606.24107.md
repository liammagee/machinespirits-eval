The scripts/drama-generator.js and poetics pipeline face a structurally analogous problem: converting an abstract dramatic plan (peripeteia arc, anagnorisis moment) into a concrete enacted transcript. DramaDirector's schema-constrained planning approach — where an LLM planner must satisfy a structural schema before generating — is a direct design pattern for adding arc-consistency constraints to the drama generator. The GRPO reward training is also relevant to the adaptation-loop: if the loop currently relies on free-form critic feedback, a geometry-analogous reward signal (e.g., "does the generated drama actually contain a recognition moment?") could make training more targeted.
