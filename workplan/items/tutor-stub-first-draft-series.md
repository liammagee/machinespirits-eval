Retroactive card, created at fold time: this ~37-iteration campaign
(2026-07-16/17) ran as dated status notes and `docs(tutor-stub): record V…`
commits without a board card. It asked whether the speaking tutor's *unaided
first draft* can satisfy the typed performance contract (owned UPTAKE /
PERFORMANCE / HANDOFF slots, deterministically audited) that V17's
character-generalization result had passed *with* the repair machinery on.

Closed result — the four-corner cage, all corners measured:

1. Model-owned form fails the audit even when blinded reviewers pass the
   semantics (V51: gerund "blaming" vs the judgment-noun ontology; 0/1 with
   two 4/4 blinded causal-fidelity reviews).
2. Harness-*repaired* form passes but is blindly preferred LESS than the raw
   rejected originals (blind review: all six rejected originals preferred over
   their delivered repairs, mean −0.60).
3. Harness-*compiled* form passes its home cell (V52 2/2; V53 Tallow 4/4) and
   fails generalization — three unseen worlds each rejected their first draft
   with three different clusters (V53).
4. Widening the recognizer to accept semantic equivalents makes the audit
   vacuous — refused prospectively by the series' own discipline (V51).

Governance record: exemplary per-iteration discipline (frozen predeclaration,
seed retirement, prospectively added gates that failed their own runs,
quarantined audit-recognition fixes, sub-loop stop rules that fired at V24–26,
V33, and per-cell at V53) — but no campaign-level card, budget, or global stop
rule; recurring clusters (causal inversion, engagement-stance, word-budget)
crossed sub-loop boundaries unpenalized. The campaign also normalized an
eight-failure test baseline, one member of which (the verbatim_learner_echo
fallback path) later crashed the Step 4 paid gate at runtime — drift debt is
not free. The fold memo's campaign-level convergence-budget and alternation
rules exist because of this record.

2026-07-17 Claude: Card created and closed at fold sanction
(PLAN_4_0/2026-07-17-continue-or-fold.md §6.1). Series honored at V53's own
terminal verdict; no paid V54; zero-call re-audit of the three saved V53
candidates proceeds as close-out documentation only. Paper landing: the
four-corner result is the utterance-grain instance of §7.11's law, written
into the fold's paper section.
