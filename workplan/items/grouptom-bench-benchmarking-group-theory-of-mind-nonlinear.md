**2026-06-24 Claude — resolved: citation-only positioning.** Cross-walk done (full
derivation in `exports/grouptom-bench-crosswalk-2026-06-24.md`). The two results sit in
different regimes: GroupToM-Bench is *group* (3+ agent), multimodal, about non-linear
collective emergence ("linear-superposition-bias"); the §6.8.5/§6.8.6 nulls are *dyadic*,
single-learner. It therefore **cannot explain** the null (no group for non-linearity to act
over → re-analysis not warranted) but **brackets** it: explicit social-mental-state structure
is inert in the dyad and expected to become load-bearing only at the group level — so the
null is a property of the dyadic regime, not rubric insensitivity (which §6.8.6 already
bounds internally). Landed as a one-sentence related-work note in §6.8.8 "what is defensible"
point 4 + ref `@tang2026grouptombench` (paper v3.0.171). No new empirical claim; no paid runs.

dynamics — GroupToM-Bench's micro→meso→macro chain is a natural scaffold for that future arc,
flagged in §6.8.8 but not scheduled. A >2-party study, if revived, is a separate
human-authorized item.
