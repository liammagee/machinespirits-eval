id-director arc.

The starting point is bounded: cell 169 is a clean local pass on two simulated
authority-refusal scenarios, and cells 170--179 show that static floors on the
two weak robustness scenarios oscillate between v2.2 instructional quality and
Weberian charisma. The next design target is a router that selects an
engagement register from learner signals, not another static charisma-floor
cell.

- Maintain the standalone plan note with DB-derived evidence.
- Keep `charisma_desire_partial_uptake` out of the primary decision rule until
  the router exists.
- Stop static-cell ablation creep after the completed cells 170--179 diagnostic.
- Define the engagement-register policy and minimal no-paid router audit before
  any new paid eval.
- Promote only a bounded claim: cells 170--179 motivate engagement-register routing;
  they do not establish a general charismatic-tutoring effect.
