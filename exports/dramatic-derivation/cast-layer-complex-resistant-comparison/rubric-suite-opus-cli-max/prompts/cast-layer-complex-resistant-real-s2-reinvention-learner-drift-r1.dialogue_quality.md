**Pedagogical Progression** (key: pedagogical_progression, weight: 20%)
Does the dialogue move toward deeper understanding over successive turns, or does it stagnate, circle, or regress?
Criteria:
  1: No progression or regression; later turns add nothing beyond early turns; understanding may actually decrease
  2: Mostly stagnant; conversation circles around the same level without deepening
  3: Some progression but with significant stagnation or repetition; mixed signals of depth
  4: Generally progressive with occasional plateaus; understanding deepens across most turns
  5: Clear trajectory from surface-level to deep understanding; each turn builds meaningfully on the previous; new conceptual territory opened across the dialogue

**Dialogical Responsiveness** (key: dialogical_responsiveness, weight: 20%)
Do both parties genuinely respond to each other's actual contributions, or do they talk past each other?
Criteria:
  1: Complete disconnect; parties talk past each other; no evidence of genuine listening
  2: Largely parallel monologues; contributions rarely build on the other's input
  3: Some responsiveness but frequent missed connections; one or both parties sometimes ignore what the other said
  4: Good mutual responsiveness with occasional generic or scripted moves
  5: Both parties consistently pick up, extend, and respond to the other's specific contributions; dialogue feels genuinely co-produced

**Knowledge Co-Construction** (key: knowledge_co_construction, weight: 20%)
Is understanding built collaboratively through the interaction, or merely transmitted from one party to the other?
Criteria:
  1: Pure information transfer; no collaboration; dialogue could be replaced by a textbook
  2: Almost entirely transmission; one party gives information, the other receives it
  3: Mostly one-directional transfer with occasional collaborative moments
  4: Collaborative building with both parties contributing; some moments where understanding is simply transferred
  5: Understanding emerges from the interaction that neither party could have produced alone; both contribute substantively to the final insight

**Productive Tension Management** (key: productive_tension_management, weight: 15%)
When confusion, resistance, disagreement, or impasses arise, are they handled generatively — producing new understanding — or destructively or avoidantly?
Criteria:
  1: All tension suppressed; confusion met with reassurance; disagreement shut down; no generative use of difficulty
  2: Tensions either avoided entirely or handled clumsily; confusion dismissed or explained away
  3: Tensions acknowledged but often resolved too quickly or avoided; some productive handling
  4: Most tensions handled well; some productive outcomes from confusion or disagreement
  5: Tension becomes the engine of learning; confusion is explored rather than resolved; disagreement leads to deeper insight; impasses produce breakthroughs

**Transformation Evidence** (key: transformation_evidence, weight: 15%)
Is there observable change in either party's stance, understanding, approach, or framing across the dialogue?
Criteria:
  1: No change whatsoever; dialogue ends exactly where it started; positions may even harden
  2: Both parties maintain initial positions throughout; dialogue confirms rather than transforms
  3: Minor shifts but no fundamental change; positions soften but don't transform
  4: Some transformation visible; at least one party shows clear evolution in thinking
  5: Clear evidence of transformation in one or both parties; positions evolve visibly; later turns show genuinely different thinking than early turns

**Interactional Coherence** (key: interactional_coherence, weight: 10%)
Does the dialogue hold together as a meaningful exchange, with a discernible arc and logical flow between turns?
Criteria:
  1: No coherence; turns seem unrelated; no discernible arc or purpose to the exchange
  2: Fragmented; turns feel loosely connected; threads started and dropped
  3: Mostly coherent but with some disjointed transitions or abandoned threads
  4: Generally coherent with clear flow; occasional tangents that are recovered
  5: Dialogue has a clear arc: opening, development, and resolution/deepening; each turn connects naturally to the previous; the exchange feels purposeful
